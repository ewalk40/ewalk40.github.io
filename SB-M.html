Description

Introducing...SimpleBackgrounds!

If you're like me and are into more simplistic things in life and you want your phone to reflect that, SimpleBackgrounds is the app for you! It features 48 flat (matte) colors to choose from and more to come!

Features:
• 24 normal flat colors
• 24 darker versions of the normal colors
• Feedback option in the settings page
• Save backgrounds as the height and width of the device you're on so no matter if you're on an iPad or an iPhone it'll always be dimensioned correctly

Features to Come:
• Custom color option
• Apple Watch app or a place in iOS app for a watch background
• Preview mode - shows what the background will look like behind apps or on the lock screen
• Many more colors
• Different color combination designs

There may be a version to come out just for Macs also so stay tuned for that!

Enjoy!

- Eric Walker, Head Developer
